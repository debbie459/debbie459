## AI Projects

### [Central Insight](https://github.com/debbie459) — Regulatory RAG for Nigerian Finance
> Plain-English intelligence tool for junior stockbrokers and analysts navigating dense CBN and SEC regulatory documents.

Built a production-grade RAG pipeline over MPC communiqués and SEC directives (2022–2024). Implemented hybrid retrieval — ChromaDB for semantic search, BM25 for keyword precision — with a cross-encoder reranker to handle specific factual queries like meeting dates and policy figures. Parent document retrieval gives the LLM full context instead of isolated chunks.

`LangChain` `ChromaDB` `BM25` `Hybrid Search` `Python` `Streamlit`

---

### [DataField Interview Prep](https://github.com/debbie459) — AI Mock Interviewer for Data Practitioners
> AI-powered mock interview platform for AI engineers, ML developers, and data scientists.

Two-LLM architecture: one model conducts the interview (HR or Technical), a second independently evaluates the user's performance using the full conversation history. Personalises questions based on role, seniority level, target company, and the user's own skills and experience. SQL-backed session management.

`LangChain` `Llama 3.3 70B` `Streamlit` `SQL` `Python`

---

### [Thinker's Scratchpad](https://github.com/debbie459) — Voice Note to Structured Idea · *[Live Demo](https://your-streamlit-link)*
> Upload a messy voice note. Get back a headline, three core arguments, an opening hook, and loose threads — without anything being written for you.

A voice-to-structure pipeline: upload any voice note → Whisper transcribes → a three-step LangChain prompt chain extracts structure from what you already said. Nothing invented, everything organised. Deployed publicly on Streamlit Cloud.

`OpenAI Whisper` `LangChain` `Groq (Llama 3.3)` `Streamlit` `Python`
